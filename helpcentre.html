Frequently Asked Questions

What is ScholarFin?

ScholarFin is a platform designed to help students access financing for their educational needs, including loans, grants, and scholarships.

How can I apply for a loan?

You can apply for a loan by creating an account on ScholarFin, completing your profile, and submitting your application through our dashboard.

What documents are required?

You will need proof of identity, academic records, and details about your financial needs. Visit the application page for a full list of required documents.
